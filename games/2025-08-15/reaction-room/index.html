Rules & Abilities

  • Board: 6×5 grid. Each round fills ~58–69% of tiles with shapes.
  • Always present: ♣, ♥, ♦, ♠ (1 each), ⭐, 🌙 (1 each), ⚡, 🛡 (2 each). The rest are basics (● ◼ ▲).
  • Goal: Click the current target shape. After every correct click, the target changes.
  • Lives: 3 total. Clicking a locked tile costs 1 life (🛡 is always safe). Wrong unlocked clicks cost 1 life.
  • Lock: One row/column is purple-locked. It moves after each correct click or any life loss.
  • Neutral tiles (empty): Click to get a hint. If it’s in the next lock’s row/col, it pulses purple; otherwise it dims until the lock moves.
  • ⚡ Lightning: Instantly reveals the next lock (pulses the row/column).
  • 🛡 Shield: Never costs a life, even when locked.
  • Exclusivity: Clicking ⭐ forbids 🌙 (and vice versa) as the next target. Clicking a suit (♣/♥/♦/♠) forbids the other suits as the next target.
  • Respawn: Every click respawns that tile into a new random shape (rules above still apply).
  • Round end: When you reach 0 lives or after 12 correct targets (your results will pop up).
